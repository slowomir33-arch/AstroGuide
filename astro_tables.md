## Pattern Definitions

### T-Square
- Structure: 2 planets in opposition, both square a 3rd (apex)
- Nature: Dynamic tension demanding action
- Apex planet: focal point of resolution
- Missing leg: area to develop

### Grand Trine
- Structure: 3 planets each 120° apart
- Nature: Natural talent circuit, can be passive
- Element: determines talent domain (fire=action, earth=practical, air=mental, water=emotional)

### Grand Cross
- Structure: 4 planets at 90° intervals (2 oppositions, 4 squares)
- Nature: Extreme tension, great drive when mastered
- Modality: determines expression style

### Yod (Finger of God)
- Structure: 2 planets sextile, both quincunx a 3rd (apex)
- Nature: Fateful mission, spiritual task
- Apex: direction of destiny

### Stellium
- Definition: 3+ planets in same sign or within 8° conjunction
- Nature: Concentrated energy, specialist focus
